## 1. Historical spine (why identity matters)

Club DJing did not start as “two faders + stems.” The lineage people cite:

| Era | Figure / shift | Brand takeaway |
|---|---|---|
| 1940s | Radio DJs → public dance spaces | Selecting for *people in a room*, not alone |
| 1960s NYC | Francis Grasso — beatmatching / continuous mix | **Craft of flow** becomes the art form |
| Later | Selector → mixer → controllerist → laptop DJ | Tools change; **reading the room** stays |

**Avoid:** centering brand lore on toxic historical figures. Prefer **craft origin** (Grasso-line continuous experience) over scandal-adjacent names.

**Deep purpose (owner language, refined):**  
A DJ is not primarily a *player of files*. A DJ is a **conductor of emotion** — selector → mixer → **experience designer** of a night’s arc.

That identity is load-bearing for brand *and* product:

| Identity | Product must do | Product must not do |
|---|---|---|
| Artist / experience designer | Amplify judgment, prep, live steer, post-set learning | Replace the DJ with silent Automix |
| Conductor of the room | Audience / energy as first-class signal | Flashy “AI DJ” hype that erases the human |
| Arc shaper | Peaks, valleys, hold, lift | Flat “recommended next track” without journey |

Aligns with Strategy: **Automix is anti-identity**; co-pilot explains + Ack; NextGen **ARRANGE** is the “design the night” surface.

## 2. Positioning hierarchy (use this order)

```text
1. WHO       Working DJs who design nights for rooms (club, festival, residency, high-stakes private)
2. JOB       Shape the emotional arc of a night under cognitive load
3. CATEGORY  Audience intelligence + journey design for DJs
             (not “another Serato”; not “ChatGPT for tracks”)
4. OUTCOME   Unforgettable nights — rooms that breathe, sets that journey
5. PROOF     Prep arcs · live steer · post-show learning · instrument trust (forked depth)
6. ENEMY     Automix cosplay · library spreadsheet panic · AI that steals the art
```

### One-sentence product promise

> **We help DJs design the night** — before, during, and after the set — without taking the decks.

### Category names to own (pick one primary)

| Category phrase | Strength | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **Audience intelligence for DJs** | Investor + product clear | “Audience” may scare privacy-first DJs unless local-first |
| **Journey design for DJs** | Poetic + ARRANGE-native | Softer commercially |
| **Night design platform** | Outcome-led | Broad; needs DJ qualifier |
| **Experience OS for DJs** | Category-creating | “OS” is tech-bro crowded |

**Recommendation:** primary **“Audience intelligence for DJs”** in decks/investors; consumer brand voice **“Design unforgettable nights.”**

## 3. Product story (sell the outcome, not the stack)

Maps 1:1 to NextGen modes + EXO — brand language should *say* this even when UI says PERFORM/ARRANGE/LIBRARY.

| Phase | DJ job | Product surface | Brand words |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pre-show** | Plan energy map, peaks/valleys, crates by *function* | LIBRARY + PREP + ARRANGE canvas | Design the arc · chapters · openers/pressure/reset |
| **Live** | Mix + decide next under load | PERFORM + one-hop ARRANGE · co-pilot why+Ack | Steer energy · hold · lift · keep the room breathing |
| **Post-show** | Learn what worked | Session insight (privacy-respecting) | Afterglow · what landed · teach the craft |

**Investor + DJ both understand:**  
*Tools that make nights better* > *models that play songs for you.*

**Technical moat (say quietly, prove loudly):**  
Cursor-path instrument depth (engine, controllers, library) + intelligence in the flow (not a bolted chat). Brand front door stays identity-first; Strategy stays Cursor-for-AI-DJing.

## 5. Name space — filter (crowded vs workable)

### Owner shortlist (from workshop) + filter

| Name | Vibe | Crowd risk | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Arc** | Journey / night shape | High (Arc browser, Arc’teryx, design tools) | Beautiful; hard to own alone → **Arc + qualifier** (Arc Booth, Arc Night) |
| **Aura** | Feeling / room energy | Medium–high (beauty, audio brands) | Soft; good sub-brand |
| **Pulse** | Energy / crowd | High (fitness, health, music blogs) | Strong metaphor; crowded |
| **Resonance** | Emotion + physics | Medium | Long; good tagline word |
| **Flowstate / Flow** | Cognitive flow | High (Flow apps) | Matches cognitive-load thesis; hard trademark |
| **Craft** | Artistry | High as solo word | Better as **verb** in manifesto |
| **Pulsecraft** | Compound, ownable | Lower | Hint audience + craft |
| **Ritual** | Night as ceremony | Medium | Strong culture fit; check religion edge |
| **Ember / Afterglow** | Memory of the night | Lower–medium | Outcome names; soft as primary |
| **Maestro / Conductor** | Role identity | Medium | “Conductor” role > product name |
| **Prism / Forge / Canvas** | Artist tools | Medium | Studio-y; less “club” |
| **AudienceOS** | Category meta | Medium | Investor-clear; hoodie-weak |
| **Migx** (current) | Technical / fork identity | Low brand meaning | Keep as **code/repo**; consumer name TBD |

### Three strategic naming paths

| Path | Move | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **A. Invent / compound** | Sonos-like inventiveness | Pulsecraft, Nightarc, Roomcraft |
| **B. Own the role** | People say “I’m a ___” | Experience architect toolkit; **Conductor** as identity not product |
| **C. Name the outcome** | What the night leaves | Afterglow, Vivid, Ember |

**Category-creating swing (owner idea, keep):**  
Coin the role — *experience architect / night designer* — and become the default tool of that identity. Bigger swing; defines space if it sticks.

### Working recommendation (for filter, not lock)

| Layer | Proposal | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Consumer brand (TBD)** | Shortlist: **Pulsecraft**, **Nightarc**, **Resonance**, **Ritual** | Ownable enough; say craft/journey not “AI mixer” |
| **Identity line** | “For DJs who design nights” | Hoodie-able; not tech |
| **Repo / eng** | **Migx** stays | Strategy, git, agents unchanged |
| **Avoid as solo global mark** | Arc, Pulse, Flow, Craft | Too crowded without modifier |

Owner picks **two vibes** (e.g. poetic craft vs crowd intelligence) → narrow to one primary + one backup.

A night is not a playlist.

It is an arc — open, tension, release, memory.
The DJ is not a jukebox. The DJ is an experience designer:
reading the room, holding energy, lifting at the right bar,
crafting journeys without words.

Tools should honor that craft.
Help plan the night. Help steer live without stealing the decks.

We build for the people who make rooms breathe.
