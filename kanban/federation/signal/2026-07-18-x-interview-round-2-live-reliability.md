## Probe script for any future live interviews (derived from X)

Use as Mom-Test probes (past behavior only):

1. Last time media failed at a gig — what exactly failed, and what did you do in the next 60 seconds?  
2. How many USBs do you walk in with, and when did that number last change?  
3. When did you last re-export because of a firmware/software update — how close to doors?  
4. Show me how you author hot cues on your controller path.  
5. Last partner/B2B set — how did music move between people (USB handoff vs anything else)?  
6. Have you ever accepted a software “next track” suggestion mid-set? What happened?
