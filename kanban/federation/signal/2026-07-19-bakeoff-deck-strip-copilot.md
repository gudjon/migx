# Bake-off unit: deck strip + co-pilot rail

**SSoT:** `kanban/knowledge/nextgen-bakeoff-deck-strip-copilot.md`

## One-line

Industry literacy = **overview+zoom waveform + transport + EQ + hot cues**; Agent DJ differentiator = **right-rail co-pilot with why + Ack-to-load** — bake them together, never chat-without-deck or silent AI DJ.

## Deck strip trends to ship

Overview/zoom pair · cue flags · Serato-clean transport · tempo visible · sync off-by-default · dark booth · stem pads later

## Co-pilot trends to ship / reject

Ship: side rail, ≤5 candidates, always **why**, Ack loads free deck only, Prep mode first.  
Reject: Spotify-style silent radio, chat covering playhead, auto-EQ writes, bolted generic chat.
