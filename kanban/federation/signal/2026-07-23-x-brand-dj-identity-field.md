# X field signal — brand/identity rooted in what DJs actually argue

**Use with:** `migx-brand-positioning-experience-designer.md` § field · `BRND-playbook` DIY-5  
**Not** a name vote — culture constraints on positioning language.

## Trend clusters (2025–2026 X)

| Cluster | What people reinforce | Brand implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Curation is sacred** | “Do we not value curation anymore?” — booking/talent discourse; playlists as identity | Category must honor **selector/artist**, not jukebox |
| **95% curation (prior high-signal)** | AI-generated setlist rejection; dug crates as legitimacy | **Enemy: silent Automix / replace the DJ** is field-true |
| **AI DJ anxiety as industry content** | DJX ’26 education: “Will AI Replace You at Your DJ Gigs?” | Homepage can **answer** with craft-first, not hide |
| **Local / no-cloud auto tools** | Indie builders: one-time purchase, on-machine, “no online AI DJs,” deterministic | Privacy + **local intelligence** is a sellable POV |
| **Spotify AI DJ ≠ club craft** | Listeners note algorithmic “DJ” chat over Joy Division | Don’t brand as **streaming AI DJ** cousin |
| **Category brands win money** | Tiny/Serato: “gold standard,” passionate users, hardware depth | Instrument trust compounds; we’re not naming into that yet |
| **Nightlife brand = energy/arc** | Club motion brands: “4 nights, 4 energies” | Visual/voice: **energy, arc, night** > SaaS purple |
| **Personal DJ brand services** | “A DJ brand that stands apart… EPK that opens doors” | Individual DJs already buy **identity as product** — we’re B2D (business-to-DJ) same job |
| **Function crates / library UX** | Crate search as workflow win (prior waves) | Product proof for “design the night” is **management**, not only decks |

## Reality checks for our drafts

| Our claim | X reality | Adjust? |
|---|---|---|
| Experience designer / conductor | Rare exact phrase; **curator / vibe / reading room / craft** dominate | Use **curator + night designer** in public; “experience designer” OK in manifesto |
| Audience intelligence | Less viral than energy/vibe; privacy-sensitive | Lead **arc/energy**; audience sensing secondary |
| Design unforgettable nights | Aligns with nightlife energy branding | Keep |
| AI co-pilot | Field split: fear of replace vs useful prep | Brand: **judgment-serving**, never “AI DJ” hero |
| Migx vs Serato | Serato = cultural gold standard in hip-hop/club English | Don’t attack Serato; **category adjacent** (journey + intelligence on deep instrument) |

## Do / don’t (field-rooted)

**Do say (X-native):** curation, craft, crates, reading the room, energy, night, journey, keep the decks, local-first optional.  
**Don’t say as hero:** “AI DJ,” “set and forget,” “we play for you,” streaming-AI-DJ parallels.
