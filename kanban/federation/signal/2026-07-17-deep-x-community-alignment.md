## 2. AI-DJing product surface (what we *ship*)

### What X is saying
**Real tools (hobby → pro-adjacent):**
- Live set matching on **BPM + key + energy + embeddings (MuQ)** turning public
  ([@mauricekleine](https://x.com/mauricekleine/status/2075612977659617305), 2026-07-10).
- Producer **co-pilot in the DAW** (Ableton): “make the crate live where they work,” stems as
  session material not destination
  ([@CoDriverAI](https://x.com/CoDriverAI/status/2073525439570817123)).
- Live stem imagination for DJ culture still resonates
  ([@levelsio](https://x.com/levelsio/status/1806396791098659272) — earlier but formative).
- Experimental **AI DJ UX** demos (branching timelines / “vibe” search)
  ([@algolia](https://x.com/algolia/status/2077878948420108545)).

**Cultural / market noise:**
- **Automix as listening UX** (djay for ADHD skip-with-transition) — not club craft
  ([@channelramblr](https://x.com/channelramblr/status/2077442019681505398)).
- **AI DJ agency / booking agents / franchise robots** — low-signal Web3/marketing
  (DjayPunks, Perfect DJ franchise, Humanoid.dj class).
- **Generative song vending** landscape mapped at scale (46 tools, ethics spectrum)
  ([@musicben_eth](https://x.com/musicben_eth/status/2021226697706639499)).
- Prior scout: pro backlash to “AI builds your setlist” remains load-bearing culture.

### Migx alignment
| Strategy pillar | X says | Board implication |
|---|---|---|
| P2 depth of permission | Co-pilot *inside* instrument wins | Session mirror + CO intents > sidebar chat |
| P3 world model | Embeddings + features used in matchers | EXO + FSL + (later) audio embeddings |
| P7 DJ-led | Craft identity vs Automix | Messaging: **Predict → Ask → Explain** |
| Anti: gen-music factory | Crowded, ethical landmines | Stay instrument + co-pilot |

**Verdict:** Moat is **not** “another Automix.” Moat is **Mixxx-grade instrument + agent-readable
set graph + human-gated intents**. Hobby embedding matchers validate EXO; do not race them on
landing pages alone.
